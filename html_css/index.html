Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe is a simple and classic guide to making soft, chewy cookies filled with rich chocolate chips.

Time: 15 minutes

Chill Time (optional): 30 minutes

Baking Time: 10–12 minutes per batch

Total Time: About 45–60 minutes

Servings: 24 medium cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups (280 g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup (170 g) unsalted butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • ¾ cup (150 g) brown sugar
  • ½ cup (100 g) white sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups (250 g) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Mix dry ingredients:
    In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Mix wet ingredients:
    In a large bowl, whisk together melted butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla:
    Whisk in the egg, then the extra yolk, and vanilla extract until combined.
  5. Combine wet and dry mixtures:
    Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture using a spatula. Mix until just combined — don’t overmix.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Chill the dough (optional): For thicker, chewier cookies, cover the dough and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  8. Shape the cookies:
    Scoop about 1½ tablespoons of dough for each cookie and place them on the tray about 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are slightly soft.
  10. Allow cookies to cool on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips for Perfect Cookies

  • Don’t overbake! Cookies continue to cook slightly after being removed from the oven.
  • For a richer flavor, use dark brown sugar instead of light brown sugar.
  • Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes for thicker, chewy cookies.
  • Use room temperature eggs for smoother mixing.
  • Add a pinch of cinnamon or sea salt on top for an extra flavor twist.
